# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want TrustReply to be a welcoming project for contributors of different backgrounds, experience levels, and perspectives.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and product decisions, not people.
- Help make the project easier for new contributors to understand.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that harms the project community.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project discussions, issues, pull requests, and other community spaces connected to TrustReply.
